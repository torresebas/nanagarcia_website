What is DelBosque?

DelBosque forest company is for everyone who love environment and nature. Understanding that the earth is one, and responsibly taking advantage of its resources is everyone's duty. Wood and its forms, leaves and ribs, water and its essence, all these elements are part of a life cycle for the existence of the human being.

Located in Colombia. It born 3 years ago, when three Forestal Engineers decided construct a company with one unique objective, responsibly take advantage of forest resources, and finally create a balance between responsible deforestation and planting.
